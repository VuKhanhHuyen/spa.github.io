ABOUT US


Welcome to Spa Beautiful

We believe everyone has the right to experience luxury, particularly when life is challenging. We believe our treatments, when supported with a range of talking therapies (life coaching, counselling, mindfulness).

We are a wellness company situated in the outskirts of Culcheth village in Warrington. The company was established in 2012 and changed into a community interest company, Wellness & Beauty For All C.I.C. known as Spa Beautiful. We are very unique in the way we approach Wellness and Beauty.

We listen to connect with you in the season of life you are in. We are here when you feel on top of the world or when you need to navigate a stressful season of life.

We have a holistic approach that aims to support your mental, physical and emotional health, on an ongoing basis, to enhance your wellness and reveal your true beauty with care and excellence.
